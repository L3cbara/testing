Popular Minecraft Mobs

Zombie
A hostile mob that spawns in the dark. Attacks players and villagers.
Skeleton
Shoots arrows at players from a distance. Burns in daylight.
Creeper
A silent and deadly mob that explodes when close to the player.
Enderman
A tall, teleporting mob that becomes hostile if you look directly at it.
Spider
A neutral mob during the day, but turns hostile at night.
